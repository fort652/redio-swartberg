Programme Rooster Program Schedule

Volgende tye is Maandag–Vrydag, tensy anders aangedui. Weekend-programme verskil.

Times below are Monday–Friday unless stated. Weekend programming differs.

  • 06:00 – 09:00 Ma–Vr Mon–Fri

    Oggendreeks

    Begin jou dag met musiek en nuus. Weer, verkeer en die beste oggend-treffers.

    Start your day with music and news. Weather, traffic and the best morning hits.

  • 09:00 – 12:00 Ma–Vr Mon–Fri

    Voormiddag

    Ligte musiek en gesels. Bel in en groet die Karoo.

    Light music and chat. Call in and say hi to the Karoo.

  • 12:00 – 15:00 Ma–Vr Mon–Fri

    Middagtreffer

    Jou gunsteling treffers deur die middag. Nuus op die uur.

    Your favourite hits through the afternoon. News on the hour.

  • 15:00 – 18:00 Ma–Vr Mon–Fri

    Namiddag

    Musiek vir die rit huis toe. Sport en gemeenskapsnuus.

    Music for the drive home. Sport and community news.

  • 18:00 – 21:00 Ma–Vr Mon–Fri

    Saans met Swartberg

    Onderhoud en gesels. Onderhoude, musiek en gesprekke met die gemeenskap.

    Entertainment and chat. Interviews, music and conversation with the community.

  • 21:00 – 06:00 Ma–Vr Mon–Fri

    Oornag

    Stilte of herhaling. Saterdag en Sondag: Weekend Vibes deur die dag.

    Overnight silence or repeats. Saturday and Sunday: Weekend Vibes through the day.

  • Saterdag & Sondag

    Weekend Vibes

    Ontspan musiek en ligte onderhoud deur die naweek. Minder nuus, meer musiek.

    Relaxing music and light entertainment over the weekend. Less news, more music.
